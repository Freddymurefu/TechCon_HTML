History

TechCon began in 2015 as a local meetup of tech enthusiasts in Nairobi. Over the years, it has grown into one of Africa’s most respected technology conferences, attracting innovators, developers, and entrepreneurs from across the globe. Each year, TechCon evolves to spotlight emerging trends, groundbreaking tools, and community-driven solutions.

Mission

Our mission is to connect, inspire, and empower the next generation of tech leaders. TechCon promotes open innovation, inclusive growth, and knowledge sharing by bringing together diverse voices from the tech ecosystem.

Past Speakers
